COVID Classroom Connections

Welcome, friends! This summer has certainly been one for the record books. Now, as the coming school year quickly approaches, so many parents, children, and their loved ones are left scrambling to prepare. There are many questions that are left for us to figure out on our own.

Here at COVID Classroom Connection we seek to bridge the gap, providing resources for families to find low-cost or free school supplies, tips on how to set up a home-classroom, and fun activities to pass the time.

Click on the images below to find resources for supplies, DIY classroom tips, and activities. Thank you for visiting our page and stay safe!
